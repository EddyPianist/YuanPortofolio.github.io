Detection Transformer
with Multi-Level DETR

In this project, we implemented DETR with Multi-Level Decoder(MLD-DETR). Multi-scale features are fused by a customized feature fusion model and we arrange them cascade in the transformer decoder. It solve the inefficiency issue at the decoder and achieves state-of-the-art performance with less computational cost.

Classical Music Classifier

The Classical Music Classifier is an AI model fine-tuned on the MAESTRO dataset to classify classical music. Built on the CLAP model, which excels in general audio tasks, it is specifically optimized for classical piano works, addressing the limitations of CLAP in this genre.
